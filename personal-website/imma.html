Irish Museum of Modern Art - Shopify Components & UX Work

Information Systems & UX Intern – Summer 2025, Dublin, Ireland

During my internship at IMMA, I updated and refined the museum’s Shopify storefront to better reflect its brand and improve the visitor experience. This included customizing templates, adjusting layout structure, and enhancing sections to make it easier for visitors to browse books, prints, and exhibition merchandise.

What I Worked On

  • Customized Shopify templates and sections using Liquid, HTML, and CSS.
  • Improved homepage layout and product hierarchy to better highlight exhibitions and seasonal items.
  • Updated collection and product components for brand consistency.
  • Optimized mobile layouts for accessibility and responsiveness.
