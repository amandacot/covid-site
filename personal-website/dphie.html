Delta Phi Epsilon – Pitt Chapter Website

Chapter Website Designer & Manager – University of Pittsburgh

For Delta Phi Epsilon at the University of Pittsburgh, I design and maintain our multi-page chapter website. The site serves as a hub for potential new members, parents, and campus partners, highlighting our values, leadership, and member experiences while staying on-brand and easy to navigate.

What I Did

  • Designed and built a multi-page Wix site to showcase our chapter’s values, leadership, and events.
  • Structured content for key audiences, including potential new members, parents, and campus partners.
  • Maintained and updated copy, photos, and layouts to keep the site current across semesters and recruitment cycles.

Chapter Website Design & Layout

I created the DPhiE Pitt chapter website to feel welcoming, organized, and easy to skim. Pages like Home, Values, Our Team, and Why I Went DPhiE give visitors a clear sense of who we are and what we care about, while keeping information structured and visually consistent across the site.
